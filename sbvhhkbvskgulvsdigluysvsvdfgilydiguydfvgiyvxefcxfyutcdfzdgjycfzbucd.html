FAQs

Q: you might be wondering, what do you all believe in? Monarchy? Social Democracy? The free market; Chirpitalism? Or are you in support of state-owned birdfeed and chirpital ideologies such as cuckooism. Wait. Are you Cuckooists? You all speak like one.

A: Well, it's quite simple really. We are a commune of birds endemic to the lands of the caucasus. I guess that--by definition makes us hard-beaked cuckooists. But we beg to differ on the idea, as it's not quite that simple as we differ quite much when it comes to the execution of cuckooism--we believe in a more free and less "state-owned" ideas than cuckooism, but not as free as what chirpitalism allows. We call this "infra-infra post-cuckooism". It's not quite so liberal as people like to think you know. Also--we're no kings. We're equal. We are a commune. While the idea of a "king" would trace back to monarchical times, yes, we refer to ourselves as "kings" to value ourselves above the monarch, while using the language of the monarch. However we're not above, or under anything.

It was a big defeat, sure, but we did not mean for this hiding to seem like cowardice. The opposite, in fact. We went to hiding to ensure the priorities of all bird-kind.
